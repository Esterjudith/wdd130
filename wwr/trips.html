Ayung River Adventure

Navigate the thrilling rapids of the Ayung River, Bali's most famous white water rafting destination. Enjoy breathtaking scenery, hidden waterfalls, and lush jungle landscapes as you paddle through Class II and III rapids, perfect for both beginners and seasoned rafters seeking an exhilarating adventure.

Telaga Waja River Challenge

Embark on a high-octane journey down the Telaga Waja River, known for its continuous Class III and IV rapids. This excursion promises heart-pounding excitement, stunning rice terrace views, and dramatic drops, making it an ideal choice for adrenaline junkies and experienced rafters seeking a challenging experience.

Melangit River Escape

Discover the hidden gem of the Melangit River, offering a more serene yet equally exciting rafting experience. Tackle Class II and III rapids amidst untouched natural beauty, ancient hanging bridges, and tropical forests. Perfect for families and those looking to enjoy a mix of adventure and tranquility in Bali's unspoiled wilderness.
